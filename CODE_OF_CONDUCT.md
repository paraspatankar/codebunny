# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in **CodeBunny** a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to a positive, safe, and productive environment for our community.

## Our Standards

- **Be respectful** – Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- **Be collaborative** – Work together constructively, and be open to feedback.
- **Be considerate** – Assume good intentions and give the benefit of the doubt.
- **Be mindful** – Recognize that tone can be misinterpreted online.
- **No harassment** – Harassment of any kind is not tolerated.

Harassment includes, but is not limited to:
- Offensive comments related to personal characteristics.
- Unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following.
- Persistent unwanted contact.
- Any other behavior that makes others feel unsafe.

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. They will address any reported violations promptly and fairly.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness any violations of this Code of Conduct, please report it to **patankarparas@gmail.com**. All reports will be handled confidentially. Violations may result in a warning, temporary ban, or permanent removal from the project, at the discretion of the maintainers.
